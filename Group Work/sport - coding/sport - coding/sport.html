Nicola Adams

A woman in boxing

  1. Nicola was born in October 1982, she retired in 2019 with an undefeated record.
  2. She comes from an African-Caribbean descent and represented England.
  3. She was the first female boxer to become an Olympic Champion.
  4. She won a gold medal in the 2012 London olympic games and in 2016, in Rio de Janeiro she also won gold.
  5. In the 2016 World Championships based in Astana, she won a gold medal.
  6. In the European Games in 2015 Baku she also won a Gold medal – first place
